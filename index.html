Connecting Comics

Connecting comics is a non-profit association dedicated to supporting comic artists at every stage of their journey. Our goal is to create a welcoming community where creators can learn, share, and grow together. By joining us, you’ll get access workshops, meet fellow artists, and develop your skills in a collaborative, inspiring environment. Whether you’re just starting out or already published, there’s a place for you here. Becoming a member is free and give you access to all our activities.
