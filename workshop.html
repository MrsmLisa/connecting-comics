Workshops

We offer a range of workshops for our members. We have beginner workshops for the person just starting and advanced workshops for the practising comic artist.

Beginner course

Comics Workshop

Join our beginner-friendly workshop and learn the basics — from turning ideas into pages, designing expressive characters, and drawing for storytelling, to creating your very own short comic. No experience needed, just bring your curiosity and a sketchbook. Come draw, learn, and meet fellow comic lovers!

  • Session 1 — Comics 101:
    From Idea to PageLearn the basics of visual storytelling, panels, gutters, and flow.
  • Session 2 — Character Design for Comics
    Create memorable characters with strong expressions and body language.
  • Session 3 — Drawing Fundamentals for Comics
    Anatomy, perspective, and simplifying forms for clear sequential art.
  • Session 4 — Short Comic
    Make a 1–2 page comic in a few hours — fun, social, and zero pressure.

Start date: March 5th

Advanced workshops

Storytelling & Writing for Comics

A hands-on workshop for writers and artists who want to tell stronger comic stories. Learn how to communicate through images alone, turn scripts into powerful pages, control pacing and page turns for emotional impact, and build rich comic worlds without info-dumping. Come sharpen your storytelling skills and create stories that really flow.

Tuesday March 10th
6pm - 9pm

Making Comics That Go Somewhere

A career-focused workshop for comic artists who want to finish projects and share their work. Learn how to manage workflows and deadlines, avoid burnout, self-publish comics and zines, grow an audience through webcomics and social media, and get valuable portfolio feedback from professionals. Turn your comic work into something ready for the world.

Tuesday April 14th
6pm - 9pm

Develope youre Art & Style

Ready to push your comic art further? This intermediate-level workshop dives into inking techniques, color and mood, finding your personal style, and using digital tools like Procreate, Clip Studio, and Photoshop. Experiment, refine your skills, and break through creative plateaus alongside fellow comic artists.

Tuesday March 24th
6pm - 9pm
