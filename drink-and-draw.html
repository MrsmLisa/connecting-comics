Drink and draw

Come join us for drinks!

Grab a drink, pick up a pen, and draw some comics with us! Join us for a relaxed Drink & Draw session where artists of all levels come together to sketch, share ideas, and have fun. No pressure, no experience required — just good vibes, comics, and creative company. Bring your sketchbook, invite a friend, and let’s draw together!

We provide coffee, tea and soda. If you want anything els you are welcome to bring it. Alcohol is allowed but you must be able to prove your age.

We meet on Fridays at 6pm.
